### Valid Placements

This plant can be placed on:

- Jungle Crux
- Nether Grass
- Nether Dirt
- Beach Crux
- Snowy Crux
- Purified Netherrack
- Desert Crux
- Stoney Crux
- Voracious Dirt
- Swampy Crux
- Basic Purified Netherrack


### Growth Rate

Every Slimefun Tick, this plant has a 9% chance to grow a stage
