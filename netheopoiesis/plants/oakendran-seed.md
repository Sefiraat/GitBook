### Valid Placements

This plant can be placed on:

- Voracious Dirt
- Nether Dirt
- Jungle Crux
- Snowy Crux
- Stoney Crux
- Desert Crux
- Nether Grass
- Beach Crux
- Swampy Crux


### Growth Rate

Every Slimefun Tick, this plant has a 4% chance to grow a stage
