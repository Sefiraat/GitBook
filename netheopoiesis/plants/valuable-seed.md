### Harvesting Tool Output

When harvested, this plant will drop: Emerald

### Valid Placements

This plant can be placed on:

Beach Crux
Purified Netherrack
Swampy Crux
Desert Crux
Nether Grass
Nether Dirt
Snowy Crux
Voracious Dirt
Stoney Crux
Jungle Crux


### Growth Rate

Every Slimefun Tick, this plant has a 7% chance to grow a stage
